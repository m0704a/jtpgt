Tpgt is a bilingual, Hungarian and English typing training program.
It makes it easy to learn to type with ten fingers.

It is recommended to practice 1 hour every day for learning.
If we practice 1 hour every day, it is about 3
months, a knowledge can be acquired with which
with high concentration on all keyboards, respectively
character is recognizable.

As you practice, make sure you are already familiar with it
do not look at the keys for characters, press
while. And our fingers, after each key pressed
return to baseline as we find it from here
the rest.
